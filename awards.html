Awards

Accolades, achievements, and recognitions.

College of Fine Arts and Communications Undergraduate Dean's List

Brigham Young University
Winter Semester (Jan 2024-Apr 2024)
  • Qualifying students are those whose grades rank in the top 5% of all CFAC undergraduates, have attained a minimum GPA of 3.5, and have completed a minimum of fourteen credit hours.
  • "Within a college that has some of the most rigorous and demanding programs on campus, you have excelled. [...] This accomplishment not only reflects well on your efforts, dedication, and self-discipline, but also evinces the positive contribution you make to your classmate cohorts and to the intellectual climate of the University." -Dean's List Letter

Stage Manager's Choice Award

Massachusetts Educational Theatre Guild
Semi-Final Round of the High School Drama Festival (March 2023)
  • One recipient is chosen by each competing site's hosting stage manager. Recipients display admirable qualities of organization, leadership, confidence, respect, and quality fulfillment of their stage management duties.

Ultimate Lifer Award

Franklin High School Theatre Company
Semi-Final Round of the High School Drama Festival (June 2022)
  • Given to a recipient whose commitment consistently rose above and beyond expectations and demonstrated long-term dedication to the company and its members.
  • Recipients not only willingly helped when needed, but actively sought out opportunities to support others in any and all ways possible.

David Dooley Award

Massachusetts Educational Theatre Guild
Regional Round of the High School Drama Festival (March 2022)
  • “This award is given to the unsung hero - someone we all felt has gone above and beyond to make this day successful, all the while receiving little fanfare for his or her hard work.” - METG David Dooley Award Speech

Scenic Design Award

Massachusetts Educational Theatre Guild
Regional Round of the High School Drama Festival (March 2022)
  • For excellence in stage and scenic design
  • Lead design and construction team in building a unique environment for actors and audience members to enjoy.
